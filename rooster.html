Rooster

The Rooster is an adult male chicken that are larger and more brightly colored than a hen (female chicken). They have a larger comb on top of their head. While Roosters do have the ability to fly, their weight hinders them from flying long distances. They can only fly about 200 ft. It's beautiful call is sure to give a great start to anyone's day.
